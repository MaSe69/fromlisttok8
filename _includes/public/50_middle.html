Turn Your Best Local Lists into Global Services

Everyday, we create lists. Most do not survive the day, but some turn out to be incredibly valuable. These lists continue their life as lists though they should proceed their way into global services. Mostly because we do not know how to perform the next step on this way. This site is meant to give you a step-by-step approach to proceed from a local list to a local program, and then from a local program to a public application.
